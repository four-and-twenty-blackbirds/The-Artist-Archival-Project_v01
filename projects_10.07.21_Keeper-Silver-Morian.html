Keeper of the Silver Morian
Ink and watercolor, with white and gold acrylic on pergamenata, 11"x7"
by Megan McC of the Barony March of Mons Tonitrus, Oct. 2021

In this example, I created a grid with a 1/2" margin, 1/8" ruled lines for calligraphy, and made space for the relevant devices being used, which are approximately 1" square.

The left and right sections were divided by a 1/4" gutter. The left section was reserved for calligraphy and a decorated capital 'K', roughly 1" square, while the right section was divided into quadrants for the artwork.
